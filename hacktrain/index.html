F.A.Q.

Hack-a-what?

Hackatrain equals a hackathon on a train, transport to Europe’s coolest technology festivals and a community of young tech talents and innovative brands. Our mission is to connect young tech-minded talents, aged 18 – 35 years, to innovative companies with the aim to help taking their business to the next level.

Can you please be more specific about Hackatrain?

OK! Hackatrain starts on a train in Amsterdam, after our arrival in Berlin the hackathon continues at Beach Bar Mitte, a funky and unusual venue. We are closing the week with a visit to Europe’s coolest technology festival Tech Open Air.

When is Hackatrain?

Hackatrain takes place on June 18 & 19. On June 20, 21 & 22 we are visiting Tech Open Air Berlin. You will return on June 23.
